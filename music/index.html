Rewrites

I often take sheet music I find and change it up because of reasons such as being too many pages.

  • One Summer's Day - Spirited Away

    • Changed from 5 to 3 pages, and some edits to make it more similar to Joe Hisaishi's live performance. Joe's performance ends on the second page. The second ending leads to the upbeat transition.

  • Test Drive - How to Train Your Dragon arr. Sebastian Wolff (made simpler)

    • Wanted to learn this piece but the original arrangement was too difficult for me. So I took out several notes, as well as a few measures during the fall I didn't like. I still find it moderately difficult.
